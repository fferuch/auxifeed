The power of responsive masonry to display your Twitter feed on your site.

== Description ==
Auxi-Feed for Twitter is a WordPress plugin that let you display your twitter feed in a WordPress page using a short code. The display follows the masonry layout and is responsive on all leading browsers. With Auxi feed you can showcase your twitter feed on your website easily .


== Installation ==

1. Download Auxi Feed (twitter version).
1. Upload the Auxi Feed directory to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etcâ€¦)
1. Activate Auxi Feed from your Plugins page.
1. Go to Settings > Auxi Feed



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

How Do I Install auxi_feed?

    Download Auxi Feed (twitter version).
    Upload the \'Auxi Feed\' directory to your \'/wp-content/plugins/\' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
    Activate Auxi Feed from your Plugins page.
    Go to Settings > Auxi Feed

Under what type of license if the plugin offered?

    GPLv2 or later
    Read it at: http://www.gnu.org/licenses/license-list.html#GPLCompatibleLicenses

What info is needed from the twitter API?

    Consumer Key (API Key)
    Consumer Secret (API Secret)
    Access Token
    Access Token Secret
    Twitter name

The information can be found here: Here's a link to [Twitter](https://apps.twitter.com// "Configure your App")

How do I add a feed to a WordPress page?

    Create a page or a post
    Add the following shortcode [auxi_feed]
    Update the page
    Done!
